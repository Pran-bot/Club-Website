G O A L S

Club is always ready to help students improve their public speaking and communication skills, and to bridge the gap between technical knowledge and the ability to articulate it. The club provides a space for students to hone their abilities in debates, group discussions, and presentations. Activities include extempore sessions and debates on topics like communalism versus capitalism. Speaking clubs can help students build confidence by providing a supportive environment to practice and refine their oratory skills, receive constructive feedback, and observe others.
